Jaipur

The Royal Pink City of India

About Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is famously known as the Pink City of India. Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, Jaipur is one of the first planned cities in India, designed with precision, symmetry, and royal elegance.

The city is a magnificent blend of royal heritage and modern life. Its pink-hued buildings, grand palaces, massive forts, and bustling bazaars make Jaipur a cultural and architectural treasure.

Forts, Palaces & Pink Dreams

Culture & Heritage

Jaipur is deeply rooted in Rajasthani traditions. Folk music, dance forms like Ghoomar and Kalbelia, vibrant festivals, and traditional attire bring the city to life. Art forms such as block printing, blue pottery, and gemstone jewelry flourish here.

The city’s bazaars are legendary. Johari Bazaar and Bapu Bazaar are famous for jewelry, textiles, handicrafts, and authentic souvenirs.

Experience Jaipur

Jaipur offers a royal experience like no other. From watching sunsets at Nahargarh Fort to exploring majestic palaces and enjoying Rajasthani cuisine, every moment here feels grand.

The city balances tourism and tradition effortlessly, making it one of India’s most loved travel destinations.
